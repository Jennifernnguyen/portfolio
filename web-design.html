(skechers)

website redesign

This project is a website redesign of Skechers, a family footwear brand featuring athletic and casual shoes in a range of styles, plus apparel. I noticed that some skechers shoes have been trending among the gen Z age group and could potentially become mainstream similar to how New Balance has in the past couple years. So I decided to redesign their website to fit the layout and aesthetic of a mainstream shoe brand whose main target audience is mid teens to twenties range.

(candl)

candle brand

This is a mockup design for Glow and Grow Club's brief 63: candl. Candl is a candle brand that heavily focuses on creating amazing and unique combinations for their scents. Their brand has a youthful, yet minimal feel. I focused on making a simple and minimalistic design that would be easy to navigate as well as being cohesive and aesthetic to look at.
