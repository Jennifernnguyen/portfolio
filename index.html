01. All about me

welcome to my portfolio website! i am a 22 years old recent college graduate from lincoln, ne with a passion for aesthetics. i love to create anything that is visually pleasing to the eye. when it comes to aesthetics i become obsessed and i experiment until i am satisfied. i enjoy designing anything from hoodies to posters to websites. i love the creative freedom that comes from it and that i am able to express myself. especially when it comes to website design, i like thinking about a user's experience, practicality, and of course the visual aspect of it. i also LOVE fashion. it's part of my everyday life and i love putting together outfits and trying out new trends. never underestimate the power of a good outfit!
